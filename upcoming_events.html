CyberPatriot


CyberPatriot is known as the National Youth Cyber Education Program that deals with cyber security and web protection. At the center of CyberPatriot is the National Youth Cyber Defense Competition, where they put teams of high school and middle school students in charge of managing the network of a small company. In the rounds of competition, teams are given a set of problems and tasks that must be completed in a six hour period. All the while hardening and trying to find ways to make it overall a better program by the end. The team with the best all around network is the winner. Here at West we have a team who happily competes and always is in it to win it! We have competitions almost every month of the year, feel free to come help and support us!
