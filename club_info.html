What do you do at the club?


Here at the Computer Science Club, one of our main goals is to find ways to access information fast and easy; whether through videos, apps, and websites. We spend our meetings programming and trying to find ways to make our current projects better. You don't need previous experience for that matter to join! You don't even need to be in a class involving code. We try and teach people how to use code to help others. If your even remotely interested in any field in technology or enginnering we highly recommened stopping by for a quick peek! Club meetings happen every Monday after school, stop by soon!
